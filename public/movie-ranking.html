The Best Movies According to Isaac

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Holes

This is a great movie and a great book! Shia Lebouff is a great actor of my time and plays this dynamic character that gets falsely accused of stealing an important pair of shoes then findshimself in a series of events somehow, remarkably connected to his family's past!

Mrs. Doubtfire

Robert Willaims (RIP) plays a dad of 3 children and gets divorced by his wife. Although a loving and caring father, having just lost his job, he is unable to claim custody of his children and he undergoes a transformation into a caring nanny that gets hired by his wife.

Great Gatsby

Based on F. Scott Fitzgerald's book, this film was produced by Jay Z and as such has impactful musical selections and great acting and story from Toby McGuire and Leonardo DiCaprio.
